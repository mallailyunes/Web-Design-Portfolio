ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Yunes Mallail. I am a Web Design student from New York City and this website is a showcase of my work in this class so far. So far, I have created my own Buzzfeed quiz, I have created an “adventure video game”. I made a website about various BJJ and judo submissions, it showcases the most common and basic ones. The website will tell you about these various way to “submit” an opponent in either a BJJ or judo match and how to perform them. My “adventure game” is about walking through a forest when you suddenly get lost. Two outcomes may happen depending on what choices you make. I have made many more things throughout my three years in this academy, but these are my two examples that I feel are my best work.
